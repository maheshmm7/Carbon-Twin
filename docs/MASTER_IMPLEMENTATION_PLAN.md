# Carbon Twin AI™

## PromptWars Challenge 3 Submission

---

# Vision

Build the most memorable Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform in the competition.

The application should not feel like a calculator.

It should feel like meeting a digital version of yourself that reveals the future impact of your lifestyle.

The experience must be emotional, visual, intelligent, and actionable.

Users should understand their carbon footprint in less than 60 seconds.

Judges should understand the value of the platform in less than 30 seconds.

---

# Core Concept

Carbon Twin AI™

A personalized digital twin that represents a user's environmental impact.

The Carbon Twin evolves based on lifestyle habits and predicts future outcomes.

The platform combines:

- Carbon awareness
- Future simulation
- AI personalization
- Behavioral change recommendations

into one seamless experience.

---

# Problem

Current carbon calculators suffer from:

- Too much manual data entry
- Boring dashboards
- Generic recommendations
- Lack of emotional engagement
- No future visualization

Most users abandon them after one use.

---

# Solution

Generate a Carbon Twin using only a few lifestyle questions.

The platform instantly creates:

1. Carbon Twin Profile
2. Future Carbon Projection
3. Green Future Projection
4. Earth Consequence Simulation
5. Personalized Action Roadmap
